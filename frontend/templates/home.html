Frequently Asked Questions

        

            

                How accurate is the AI Resume Screener?
                
Our AI-powered screener utilizes advanced Gemini models and Sentence Transformers for both ATS compatibility and semantic understanding. While highly accurate, it's designed to assist, not replace, human decision-making. We continuously refine our models for improved precision.

            

            

                Can HR upload multiple resumes at once for shortlisting?
                
Absolutely! Our platform features a Bulk Resume Shortlister, allowing HR professionals to upload hundreds of resumes simultaneously against a single job description. The AI then processes and ranks them, providing an efficient and unbiased shortlist.

            

            

                What kind of feedback do candidates receive?
                
Candidates receive immediate, point-wise, AI-powered feedback on their resume's ATS score and semantic fit. This includes suggestions for missing keywords, identified skill gaps, and general content optimization to help them tailor their application effectively.

            

            

                Is the platform secure for sensitive data?
                
We prioritize data security. While specific security protocols would be detailed in a full deployment, the architecture is built with industry best practices in mind, using robust backend technologies like Flask and SQLite for secure data handling and user authentication.
