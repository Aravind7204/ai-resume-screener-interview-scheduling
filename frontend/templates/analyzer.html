What do the scores mean?

Our evaluation employs a balanced, two-pronged approach to assess your resume's job fitness. Semantic Matching (weighted at 60%) measures the deep, conceptual alignment between your career history and the job's core responsibilities. Keyword Density (weighted at 40%) ensures technical compliance by counting the presence of exact skills and terms from the job description.

A low score, such as your 35%, is a clear warning sign that requires immediate action. While your profile achieves a moderate conceptual fit, the resume currently suffers from a significant deficit in specific, high-value technical terms. It lacks the explicitly demanded keywords like PHP, MVC, and OOP, which are central to the role. To successfully pass the screening phase and reach a human reviewer, you must prioritize integrating these crucial terms.
